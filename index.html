    Scenario math

    Each day: previous balance × odds (full re-stake). Day 1 uses your bankroll as the starting amount.

    Each “step” or “day” here means one winning leg at the stated odds. Tick Done when you’ve completed that day’s leg (saved with your plan). Real outcomes are not guaranteed.

    Stay in control

    • Cap selections: avoid stacking more than three picks on one ticket.
    • Treat this tab as a calculator, not a reason to chase losses.
    • Patience: small, rare stakes beat all-in pressure.
    • If it stops feeling fun, pause and use BeGambleAware (UK).

    Data on this device

    Your numbers update and save as you type - no separate Save button. Your plan, profile photo, and sign-in token live in this browser’s storage only. There is no server: nothing is sent to the cloud, and there is no password reset flow. Publishing the app on GitHub Pages only hosts the public files (HTML/JS/CSS); it does not store your numbers. Closing the app or tab on your phone usually keeps your data; you lose it if you clear site data, uninstall the browser, or use a different device unless you restore a backup.

    Delete account removes this user’s data from this browser.

    Email labels your encrypted password hash on this device only. No server, no sync, no reset; use Export backup if you might replace the phone or clear storage.
